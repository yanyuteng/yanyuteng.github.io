Blogs of Sociologists

Posted on 2018-04-18   |   In Prose   |   This article has been read times

Actually, I wish to collect the sociologists’ blog sites have been long. This essay collects blogs that are still updated now, I hope this collection is helpful for anyone who reading this essay now.

USA
kieran healy
Brief introduction: Associate Professor of Sociology at Duke University.

Yuqiong Li
Brief introduction: Master student at New York University Center of Data Science. A graduate student in sociology once, working on social movements and cultural sociology.

施永仁
Brief introduction: Associate Professor of Sociology at University of Iowa.

England
Mark Carrigan
Brief introduction: A researcher in the Culture Politics and Global Justice cluster in the Faculty of Education at the University of Cambridge. He tends to describe himself as a digital sociologist.

张悦悦/Joy
Brief introduction: Senior Lecturer／Associate Professor in Sociology at University of Kent

Elder
Brief introduction: A Reader in the Department of Social Sciences at Loughborough University

Australia
This Sociological Life, Deborah Lupton
Brief introduction: Centenary Research Professor in the Faculty of Arts & Design, University of Canberra

Sweden
Cheng Lin
Brief introduction: Master student at LiU

Taiwan
曾柏文
Brief introduction: Albert Tzeng was born in Taipei and moved seven times before he was seven years old. He spent his childhood in Taichung from elementary school to high school. Tzeng transferred from the Department of Chemistry to the Department of Psychology at Taiwanese University. He then went to LSE to study sociology. I am very interested in his doctoral thesis ‘Framing Sociology in Taiwan, Hong Kong and Singapore’ at Warwick University.

張育誠
Brief introduction: Yucheng is a master student majoring in sociology at Taiwan University. He is my classmate in the course of social statistics taught by Professor Gaohua Ye in 2016.

Mainland
王潇宇
Brief introduction: A bachelor in Yunnan Normal University.

But some blogs are not written by individuals. These blogs are co authored by a group of sociologists, as shown below.

USA
Everyday sociology
Brief introduction: Karen Sternheimer teaches in the sociology department at the University of Southern California; Sally Raskoff has been employed as a sociologist since the mid 1980s and is currently the Chair of Sociology and Ethnic Studies at Los Angeles Valley College; Peter Kaufman; Teresa Irene Gonzales…… etc.

The Society Pages
Brief introduction: The Society Pages (TSP) is an open-access social science project headquartered in the Department of Sociology at the University of Minnesota and supported by individual donors

England
Sociological imagination
Brief introduction: Mark Carrigan, Milena Kremakova, Sadia Habib

Taiwan
巷仔口社會學
Brief introduction: A blog which written by the group of Taiwan sociologists

菜市場政治學
Brief introduction: A blog which written by the group of Taiwan politicians

Mainland
社会学吧
Brief introduction: Held by the NJU
