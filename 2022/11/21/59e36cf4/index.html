Memo_稳定人口各指标变动影响

Posted on 2022-11-21 In Demography Views: Waline: Words in article: 1.6k Reading time ≈ 3 mins.

按查瑞传《数理人口学》、𫖮菲兹《应用数理人口学》，就稳定人口各指标变动的推导作简要记录。

一、稳定人口基本方程

$$
\begin{aligned}
&b=\frac{1}{\int_0^\omega e^{-r a} l(a) d a} \qquad(1) \\
&c(a)=b e^{-r a} l(a) \qquad(2) \\
&\psi(a)=\int_\alpha^\beta e^{-r a} l(a) \phi(a) d a=1 \qquad(3) \\
&l(a)=e^{-\int_0^a \mu(a)} d a \qquad(4) \\
\end{aligned}
$$

二、$\phi(a)$ 变动影响

1. $\phi(a)$ 等比例变动对r的影响

$$
\begin{aligned}
&\because \phi^{\prime}(a)=e^{k a} \phi(a) \\
&1=\int_\alpha^\beta e^{-r^{\prime} a} l(a) \phi^{\prime}(a)
=\int_\alpha^\beta e^{-r^{\prime} a} l(a) \phi(a) e^{k a}
=\int_\alpha^\beta e^{-\left(r^{\prime}-k\right) a} l(a) \phi(a) \\
&1=\int_\alpha^\beta e^{-r a} l(a) \phi(a) d a=1 \qquad(3) \\
&\therefore -\left(r^{\prime}-k\right)=r \\
&r^{\prime}=r+k \\
&\phi^{\prime}(a) \uparrow \quad k>0 \quad r^{\prime} \uparrow \\
&\phi^{\prime}(a) \downarrow \quad k<0 \quad r^{\prime} \downarrow
\end{aligned}
$$

2. $\phi(a)$ 某一岁变动对r的影响

$$
\begin{aligned}
&\because e^x \doteq 1+x ,\quad e^{-\Delta r a} \doteq (1-\Delta r \cdot a) \\
&\therefore e^{-(r+\Delta r) a} = e^{-r a} \cdot e^{-\Delta r a}
= e^{-r a}(1-\Delta r a)
\end{aligned}
$$

$$
\begin{align}
&1=\int_\alpha^\beta e^{-(r+\Delta r) a} l(a)[m(a)+\Delta m(x)] d a \\
&\space\space=\int_\alpha^\beta e^{-(r+\Delta r)} l(a) m(a) d a+e^{-(r+\Delta r) x} l(x)\Delta m(x) \\
&\space\space=\int_\alpha^\beta e^{-r a}(1-\Delta r a) l(a) m(a) d a+C \\
&\space\space=\int_\alpha^\beta e^{-r a} l(a) m(a) d a-\Delta r \int_\alpha^\beta e^{-r a} l(a) m(a) a+C \\
\end{align}
$$

$$
\begin{aligned}
&1-\Delta r-\mu+C=1 \\
&C=\Delta r \cdot \mu \\
&\Delta r \cdot \mu = e^{-(r+\Delta r) x} l(x) \Delta m(x) = e^{-r x}(1-\Delta r x) l(x) \Delta m(x) = e^{-r x} l(x) \Delta m(x) \\
&\Delta r =\frac{e^{-r x} f(x)}{\mu} \Delta m(x)
\end{aligned}
$$

此外有$l(a)$、$NRR$、$T$等变动影响请参见笔记（不想打公式了）。
