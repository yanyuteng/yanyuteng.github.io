二、ϕ(a)\phi(a)ϕ(a) 变动影响

1. ϕ(a)\phi(a)ϕ(a) 等比例变动对rrr的影响

ϕ′(a)=ekaϕ(a)1=∫αβe−r′al(a)ϕ′(a)da  =∫αβe−r′al(a)ϕ(a)ekada  =∫αβe−(r′−k)al(a)ϕ(a)da1=∫αβe−ral(a)ϕ(a)da=1(3)−(r′−k)=rr′=r+kϕ′(a)↑k>0r′↑ϕ′(a)↓k<0r′↓\begin{aligned} &\phi^{\prime}(a)=e^{k a} \phi(a) \\ &1=\int_\alpha^\beta e^{-r^{\prime} a} l(a) \phi^{\prime}(a) d a \\ &\quad\ \ =\int_\alpha^\beta e^{-r^{\prime} a} l(a) \phi(a) e^{k a} d a \\ &\quad\ \ =\int_\alpha^\beta e^{-\left(r^{\prime}-k\right) a} l(a) \phi(a) d a \\ &1=\int_\alpha^\beta e^{-r a} l(a) \phi(a) d a=1 \qquad(3) \\ &-\left(r^{\prime}-k\right)=r \\ &r^{\prime}=r+k \\ &\phi^{\prime}(a) \uparrow \quad k>0 \quad r^{\prime} \uparrow \\ &\phi^{\prime}(a) \downarrow \quad k<0 \quad r^{\prime} \downarrow \end{aligned}​ϕ′(a)=ekaϕ(a)1=∫αβ​e−r′al(a)ϕ′(a)da  =∫αβ​e−r′al(a)ϕ(a)ekada  =∫αβ​e−(r′−k)al(a)ϕ(a)da1=∫αβ​e−ral(a)ϕ(a)da=1(3)−(r′−k)=rr′=r+kϕ′(a)↑k>0r′↑ϕ′(a)↓k<0r′↓​

2. ϕ(a)\phi(a)ϕ(a) 某一岁变动对rrr的影响

ex≐1+x,e−Δra≐(1−Δr⋅a)e−(r+Δr)a=e−ra⋅e−Δra  =e−ra(1−Δra)\begin{aligned} &e^x \doteq 1+x ,\quad e^{-\Delta r a} \doteq (1-\Delta r \cdot a) \\ &e^{-(r+\Delta r) a} = e^{-r a} \cdot e^{-\Delta r a} \\ &\quad\ \ = e^{-r a}(1-\Delta r a) \end{aligned}​ex≐1+x,e−Δra≐(1−Δr⋅a)e−(r+Δr)a=e−ra⋅e−Δra  =e−ra(1−Δra)​

1=∫αβe−(r+Δr)al(a)[m(a)+Δm(x)]da  =∫αβe−(r+Δr)al(a)m(a)da+e−(r+Δr)xl(x)Δm(x)  =∫αβe−ra(1−Δra)l(a)m(a)da+C  =∫αβe−ral(a)m(a)da−Δr∫αβe−ral(a)m(a)a+C\begin{aligned} &1=\int_\alpha^\beta e^{-(r+\Delta r) a} l(a)[m(a)+\Delta m(x)] d a \\ &\quad\ \ =\int_\alpha^\beta e^{-(r+\Delta r) a} l(a) m(a) d a+e^{-(r+\Delta r) x} l(x)\Delta m(x) \\ &\quad\ \ =\int_\alpha^\beta e^{-r a}(1-\Delta r a) l(a) m(a) d a+C \\ &\quad\ \ =\int_\alpha^\beta e^{-r a} l(a) m(a) d a-\Delta r \int_\alpha^\beta e^{-r a} l(a) m(a) a+C \end{aligned}​1=∫αβ​e−(r+Δr)al(a)[m(a)+Δm(x)]da  =∫αβ​e−(r+Δr)al(a)m(a)da+e−(r+Δr)xl(x)Δm(x)  =∫αβ​e−ra(1−Δra)l(a)m(a)da+C  =∫αβ​e−ral(a)m(a)da−Δr∫αβ​e−ral(a)m(a)a+C​

1−Δr⋅μ+C=1C=Δr⋅μΔr⋅μ=e−(r+Δr)xl(x)Δm(x)  =e−rx(1−Δrx)l(x)Δm(x)  =e−rxl(x)Δm(x)Δr=e−rxl(x)Δm(x)μ\begin{aligned} &1-\Delta r \cdot \mu+C=1 \\ &C=\Delta r \cdot \mu \\ &\Delta r \cdot \mu = e^{-(r+\Delta r) x} l(x) \Delta m(x) \\ &\quad\ \ = e^{-r x}(1-\Delta r x) l(x) \Delta m(x) \\ &\quad\ \ = e^{-r x} l(x) \Delta m(x) \\ &\Delta r =\frac{e^{-r x} l(x) \Delta m(x)}{\mu} \end{aligned}​1−Δr⋅μ+C=1C=Δr⋅μΔr⋅μ=e−(r+Δr)xl(x)Δm(x)  =e−rx(1−Δrx)l(x)Δm(x)  =e−rxl(x)Δm(x)Δr=μe−rxl(x)Δm(x)​​
